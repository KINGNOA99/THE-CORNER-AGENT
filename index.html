PROMOTION PHASE ROADMAP
👀
AWARENESS · 4+ weeks out
Introduce your brand/business. Share your story, why you started, and what makes you different. Focus on organic reach.
🔥
INTEREST · 2–4 weeks out
Show your products/services in action. Behind-the-scenes, customer testimonials, price lists. Build trust.
⭐
DESIRE · 1–2 weeks out
Limited offers, flash sales, countdowns. Create urgency. "Only 3 slots left this week!"
📣
ACTION · Final week
Strong CTAs: "Book now", "DM to order", "Visit us today". Make it easy to buy.
🚀
RETAIN · After sale
Thank customers, ask for reviews, share user-generated content. Turn one buyer into a regular.
